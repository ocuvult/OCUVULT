After you have registered and logged in, open your wallet to buy, send, and receive Ocuvult.

Secure
It is impossible to lose Ocuvult stored inside
of Ocuvult's user registration system blockchain. Even
if the software and hardware that builds
Ocuvult is destroyed, the creator will
rebuild the user account blockchain
with the original amount of Ocuvult
held by every user.
Decentralized
Ocuvult is as decentralized as it's users make it.
You want to create a wallet and install it on a toaster?
Great! just submit the executable and we will provide a
receiving address for the wallet and Ocuvult to pay
you for your contributions. Ocuvult that leaves the user
account blockchain becomes the responsibility
of the owner of the receiving external wallet and cannot
be sent back to the user account blockchain. There
is no guarantee that external wallets and the amount
of Ocuvult on them will be restored if they are
compromised.
Confirmed
Every send order that you make from your account is confirmed
by you through email. This makes it impossible for your ocuvult
to be stolen if your account is compromised.
