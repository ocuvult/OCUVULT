After you have registered and logged in, open your wallet to buy, send, and receive Ocuvult.

Secure
It is impossible to lose Ocuvult stored inside
of Ocuvult's user account blockchain. Even
if the software and hardware that builds
Ocuvult is destroyed, the application will
be rebuild itself with the original amount
of Ocuvult held by each user.
Mine
Ocuvult is as easy to mine as it is to register an account.
Every registered account generates 0.05 Ocuvult.
After 24 hours or a purchase of more Ocuvult,
the mined Ocuvult can be sent to other users.
Confirmed
Every send order that you make from your account wallet is confirmed by you through email. This makes it impossible for your Ocuvult to be stolen even if your account wallet is compromised.
